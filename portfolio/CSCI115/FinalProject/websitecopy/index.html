Welcome

Of all the chapters in life, college is one of the most important for an individual's journey to a healthy, successful life. It is the biggest leap in personal growth, and can become very challenging at times. This site will teach you steps towards academic success, as well as balance the big changes in lifestyle all at once. Included are tips, guides, and sources for creating and maintaining a healthy and successful lifestyle through the college years. Some of the ideas or habits may seem difficult to manage, but through practice and patience, you will be well on your way to becoming the best version of yourself!

College is a time for dropping old habits and creating new ones. Somtimes old habits and baggage have to be dropped in order to move past a certain point in life, and college is often that point. We can think of it as a doorway, and the weight on your shoulders - whether it be guilt, fears, bad habits, - is your baggage. Often times the doorway is too small, and the weight you carry is too large for you continue. College is a time where you can drop that weight, and leave it all behind as you enter into the next chapter of your life.
