Informational Website: Planning Analysis

Website Goals
The purpose of this website is to guide students on how to live a healthy college lifestyle. It will cover the main aspects of physical and mental health, while keeping a focus on academics. It will include some of the more crucial tips for success, as well as some specifics for each catagory or focus. These categories include organization and time management, personal growth, academics, mental wellness, and physical health.
Web host and URL:
Because this will not be a commercial site, I will not be using a host outside of the CofC student web domain.
Navigation menu
  • Home - A welcome page giving an overview of the site and its content
  • Survival Guide - Tips for surviving college academics
  • Mind - This page will be dedicated to mental wellness
  • Body - Likewise, this will be dedicated to maintaining physical health
  • Inspiration - This page will include insipring quotes, videos, and other media
Navigation menu is subject to change
Information and recources
For this website I will be needing plenty of images, which I will most likely be using from other sites through google images. I will be also pulling other helpful information such as healthy eating information from other informative sites. This may include research articles or other publications.
